Dice generates states between ONE and SIX based on a random value.
Raw Data is a randomValue used to compute the output state.

# States:

- `ONE`
- `TWO`
- `THREE`
- `FOUR`
- `FIVE`
- `SIX`

# Raw Data:

`randomValue (double)`
